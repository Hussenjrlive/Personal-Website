About Me

My Journey

From student to builder — how I ended up creating real products before finishing college.

I always wanted to be part of the tech world, but I never imagined I'd be building software before finishing college. I met my teammates at a point where we were all figuring things out. We decided to create REZ Group together and started working on real projects. None of us had it all figured out — we learned as we built.

I was intimidated at first. I didn't know where to start, and I doubted myself at every turn. But I took the first step anyway. I learned that nothing gets spoon-fed — you have to fight for it. The highs and lows taught me more than any classroom could. That fear of starting? It never fully goes away. You just learn to move with it.

What started as a group of friends became something bigger. We went from being friends to family. We pushed each other, supported each other, and grew together. Today, I'm Co-founder of REZ Group, and I build mobile apps and websites for businesses worldwide. We all started building before we were "ready," and that's exactly why we're ahead now.
